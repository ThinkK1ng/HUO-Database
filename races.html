Races of Neova

The races of Neova are varied and strong, each of them offering unique benefits. Though there are some places where coexistance thrives,
Not all interactions are clean and just. War is not unheard of, and, unfortunately it does them no favors against the greater Gogmagog threat.

Human

Alone, humans are not as threatening as some of the other races, but they are the most abundant. Built in the image of the goddess, they are the closest to her realm.
Racial SKill: +20% exp gain and 30% increased faith skill damage.

Elf

Though elves have the second smallest population amongst all the races, numbering only in the thousands, they make up for this with their immense magical knowledge and longevity. The races with the longest lifespan, some elves have been known to live well past two thousand years.
Racial Skill: +100 MP and 30% increased magic skill damage.
