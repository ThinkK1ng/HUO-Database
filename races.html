Races of Neova

The races of Neova are varied and strong, each of them offering unique benefits. Though there are some places where coexistance thrives,
Not all interactions are clean and just. War is not unheard of, and, unfortunately it does them no favors against the greater Gogmagog threat.

Human

Alone, humans are not as threatening as some of the other races, but they are the most abundant. Built in the image of the goddess, they are the closest to her realm.

Racial Trait - Arianrhod's Guidance: +20% exp gain and 30% increased faith skill damage. 10% increased damage taken from magic skills.

Elf

Though elves have the second smallest population amongst all the races, numbering only in the thousands, they make up for this with their immense magical knowledge and longevity. The races with the longest lifespan, some elves have been known to live well past two thousand years, marking them as the wisest of all races and scholars like no other.

Racial Trait - Forest's Blessing: +100 MP and 30% increased magic skill damage. 10% increased damage taken from shadow skills.

Dragonkin

Prideful and mighty, the Dragonkin are a curious race. Once Human, they made pacts with the ancient dragons and were bestowed with their might. Their population grew from there, and though they are only 7 thousand strong to this day (the smallest population of Neova, in fact), they live nearly as long as the elves. They are almost indistiguishable from humans, if not for their slited golden eyes.

Racial Trait - Dragon Force: 10% increased faith skill damage, 15% increased elemental skill damage, 15% increased physical skill damage and acess to a Dragon Form. Dragon form has a duration of 100 seconds (10 replies) in which all stats are increased by 100% and has a cooldown of 600 seconds (60 replies). 20% increased damage taken from all sources.

Orc

Once a bitter enemy to the human race, while not overly friendly, Orcs are now considered allies thanks to the peaceweaving of their greatest past leader Sorg. Taller and more muscled than any other race, the only ones that come close to them in physiacl ability are the Dragonkin. Their thick skin makes them the most durable of the races, making Orcs hard to bring down.

Racial Trait - Sorg's Might: 30% increased defense and physical skill damage.

Vampire

Humans who were tainted by the evils of the Gogmagog, they are a race divided. Some wish for Neova to fall, others hate what has been done to them and wish the Gogmagog to die. Fully capable of sucking the life from all those around them, Vampire's are a frightening foe to have.

Racial Trait - Voracity: 10% increased shadow skill damage and 60% increased self healing. +10 points of additional damage added to skills for every stack of Ravenous. 1 ravenous stack gained per enemy killed, 15 stacks max. 20% increased damage taken from faith skills.

Beastman

The Beastmen are the scond most prominent race on Neova, numbering nearly as many oas the humans they govern themselves in their own kingdoms. Tensions between them and the humans tend to be non-existent and they enjoy the peace and trade with them. Their relations with the Orcs, however, are even better.

Racial Trait - Instinct: 30% increased agilty, 15% increased recon skill range and duration, and night vision.

Dwarf

Short in stature but brawny and smart, the dwarves are the greatest engineers of Neova. They are third largest race in the land and have been responsible for the vast majority of Neova's technological advances alongside the elves. They have a healthy rivalry there. Almost polar opposites of the elves, the Dwarves prefer to live in hot mountains so they have plenty of raw material available to them for their forges.

Racial Trait - Magni's Hammer: 30% increased fire skill damage and 10% increased physical skill damage. 10% increased damage taken from water skills.
